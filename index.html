About myself

My name is Valeria, I am a 3rd year student of the Lviv National Academy of Arts. Studying to be a graphic designer, specializing in illustration.

In my free time from the academy, I try to pay attention to my hobbies, namely ballet and posing for photographs.

I dream of my own spacious gallery where young talented artists can exhibit. And I also want my gallery to become a place where adults and children can come for spiritual development, inspiration, or just for peace of mind and satisfaction.
